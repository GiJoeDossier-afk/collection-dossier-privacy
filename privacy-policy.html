Collection Dossier — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Collection Dossier ("the app") is a G.I. Joe Classified Series figure collection tracker. This page explains what data the app collects and how it's used.

What we collect

  • Email address — used only to create and sign in to your account, via Firebase Authentication (a Google service).
  • Collection status — which figures you've marked as ACQUIRED. This is stored against your account so it's available if you reinstall the app or sign in on another device.

What we don't collect

Photos you attach to figures stay on your device only and are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

Aggregate metrics

The app shows a "Metrics" view with the percentage of users who own each figure, calculated from anonymised counts across all accounts. Individual collections are never shown to other users — only the combined totals.

Data storage

Account and collection data is stored using Firebase (a Google Cloud service). It is not sold or shared with any other third party.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from inside the app: open the menu and select "Delete Account". This immediately removes your account, your collection record, and your contribution to the aggregate metrics.
